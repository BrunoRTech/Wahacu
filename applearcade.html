Apple Arcade

Apple Arcade is a service included in the App Store. It was launched in 2019 and offers mobile games which are only available in Apple Arcade and don’t contain ads. It’s based on the subscription model where the new user has the first month of Apple Arcade for free. After the free trial, the individual subscription costs $4.99 per month.
