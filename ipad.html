iPad

iPad is the line of Apple's tablets. The first iPad was launched at April 3, 2010. The goal of creating the iPad was to create something between iPhone and MacBook. Currently, Apple releases 3 lines of iPads: the normal one which is not specially named, Air, the cheaper one, and Pro, the most expensive and the most advanced model from all of the types.

I don't want to create the second Wikipedia so if you want to see all of the iPads launched by Apple, check this Wikipedia article.
