TIMELINE WITH THE MOST APPLE'S IMPORTANT MOMENTS


1976

April 1, 1976, day when the company was founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne. First logo was too complicatted for the actual times hovewer it was perfectly connected with the company's name. Their purpose was making the computers.


1977

Apple Computer changed their logo. In my opinion, to the better one, less complicated and easier to remember. And you, what do you think?


1985

The company develops. There were new types of computers launched. We can think: "What could go wrong?". To understand the situation we need to go back to 1983 when John Sculley, who in past helped Pepsi succeed, joined Apple Computer. Two years later, in 1985, Apple Computer planned the presentation of its new computer which was planned as the successor of unsuccessful LISA computer. But there was one element that arose the difference between Jobs and Sculley: the price. Two different prices, two different strategies. Sculley said that the computer should cost 2500$ to make up losses. Jobs said that the computer should cost 2000$ because if it’ll cost more, it won’t be chosen by the consumers. The majority of Apple Computer employers were agreed with John Sculley, which prompted Steve Jobs to leave Apple Computer and start his own new company called NeXT. In my opinion, Jobs’s thinking was correct. And you, what is your opinion?

1987

Apple Computer was one of the first companies with its internet domain. In present times it’s not anything unique. But in 1987 it was something new. It was the beginning of the Internet.

1996

Apple Computer took control of Jobs’s company, NeXT. It meant that Jobs was coming back to the Apple company after 11 years of “break”.

1997

After the unsuccessful attempts to launch other types of devices besides computers, Apple Computer fell into a crisis. Apple Computer to not to go bankrupt decided to make the deal with Microsoft, one of the biggest if not the biggest Apple Computer’s opponents. In return for the investment in Apple Computer by Microsoft, Apple Computer needed to implement Microsoft Office and Internet Explorer in its computers. That deal helped Microsoft promote its services. In fact, if Microsoft wouldn’t help, Apple Computer would collapse.


1998

Apple Computer presented first computer from the new line: iMac, which was the computer all-in-one and to work it just needed two cables, power cable and internet cable. It’s not a surprise that this computer was successful. Also in the same year Apple Computer changed their logo.

2001

The new 21st century started by 3 important novelties: Mac OS X operation system, which defined the next editions of operation system for Macs; the revolutionary portable music player iPod and the first physical Apple Store in Virginia, US. Also in the same year Apple Computer changed their logo.

2006

Apple Computer in their computers started using Intel processord instead IBM processors. It wasn't a bad decission, especially because it was the period when IBM started focus more on services instead devices. At the same year Apple launched MacBook, the successor of other line of laptops, iBook.

2007

New 3 things was presented: first iPhone 2G, first Apple TV, and the new logo. Also, the name of the company was changed from Apple Computer to Apple, to show the new plan of the company which is not producing the computers. Another thing is that it the name wasn't changed in 2001 when Apple started producing iPod. However, in my opinion change the name was a good decision.


2010

Apple presented its first tablet, iPad.

2011

Steve Jobs, one of 3 founders of Apple company, died. It was a sad day for all of the people who admire the merits of him.


2015

Apple presented its first smartwatch named Apple Watch.

2017

Apple changed the logo sixth time. In my opinion, the actual logo is the best one. And you, what do you think?
