PLANS FOR FUTURE: APPLE'S MOST KNOWN PROBABLE PRODUCTS OF THE FUTURE

Smart Glasses/
VR Glasses

Some time ago, a thread appeared in the world's media informed about intelligent glasses and VR glasses. It couldn’t be a surprise if we’ll take into account fact that also Google worked on plan for smart glasses and another fact that there are already tons of VR glasses on the market. There are already a lot of VR glasses on the market, so the entry of Apple on this market would rather not surprised anyone. In my opinion, Apple’s smart glasses (which could be named “iGlass” or “Apple Glass”), if it’ll be created, it won’t be the device which will replace iPhones, but it would be the complement of the iPhone and for example show the most important informations, like Apple’s smartwatch named Apple Watch. About VR glasses (which could be named “iVR” or “Apple VR”), I’ll see it as the enlargement to Apple’s set-top box for the TV named Apple TV.

Car

For some people it could be a surprise but in past in media we could see lots of articles about potential Apple’s car (which could be named “iCar” or “Apple Car”). It wouldn’t be a bad marketing idea but I still have a question: How would Apple's car be unique among other cars on the market?
