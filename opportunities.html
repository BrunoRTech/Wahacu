OPPORTUNITIES

Apple, same as other big companies, is criticized. Some people hate Apple, same as some people adore this company.
Below you’ll find my opinion about two elements of Apple’s strategy which are most often criticized.

Prices

Compared to competitors' products, the prices of Apple devices are too high. In my opinion, they could be lower. But probably soon this won’t be changed if we will take into account the faithful group of fans which will buy everything of what was launched by Apple.

Apps only from App Store

The second element which is sometimes criticized by the audience, most users of the Android operation system, is the lack of possibility to download apps outside Apple’s service with apps App Store. Apple argues this to reduce the chances of catching a virus on the device, which I support. In addition, the strategy of Apple was always to provide easy in use products.
